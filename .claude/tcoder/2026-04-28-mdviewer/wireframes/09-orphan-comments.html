RFC-031: Per-tenant Rate Limiting

Summary

Across Q1 we observed two incidents where one customer's traffic burst pushed gateway p99 from 80ms to over 1.2s, affecting every other tenant on the shared infrastructure. This RFC proposes a per-tenant token-bucket limiter at the gateway.

Design

Each gateway pod owns a local token bucket per (tenant, route-class) pair, replenished from a global ledger every refresh_interval seconds.

Operational concerns

Lua scripts are versioned and shipped via the config service.
